What is Blogry?

Blogry is the site that provide an opportunity to various talented people out there who can write blogs but face problems in writing due to professional or personal reasons. The idea behind the blogry was to provide a social platform to the people who can write or express their feelings, opinions and thoughts in a form of blog with the people all over the world.

Blogry came into existence on 6th June 2020 as a normal blog website, later on 9th november 2020 it was change into a blogging platform where each and every person can write a blog for free and can earn money through blogs.

What is the aim behind Blogry?

The main aim behind blogry is to provide people a platform where they can express their thoughts freely in a form of blog and where they can earn money through those blogs. In Blogry people can promote their businesses,there affiliate links, there YouTube videos and much more through the blogs.
